Dumpling Dipping Sauce

This dipping sauce for asian style dumplings is the bomb dot com

Author: Meg Russ

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil (I sub with spicy chili crisp)
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions

  1. Dissolve the sugar in the hot water
  2. Combine sugar-water and all other ingredients in a bowl
  3. Enjoy with your favorite dumplings! :)

Enjoy!
