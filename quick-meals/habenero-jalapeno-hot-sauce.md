# Habenero Jalapeno Hot Sauce

![Habenero Jalapeno Hot Sauce](images/habenero-jalapeno-hot-sauce.jpeg)

Smoky spicy hot homemade hot sauce great for tacos, soups, and chicken.  A little goes a long way.

**Author:** Habenero Jalapeno Hot Sauce

**Serves:** 1

## Ingredients

* * 1 tbsp olive oil
* * 10 habanero peppers, diced
* * 2 jalapeno peppers, diced
* * 1 tabasco pepper, diced
* * Half of a tomato, diced
* * 8 garlic cloves
* * 1 shallot, chopped
* * ½ cup water
* * ¾ cup apple cider vinegar
* * 2 tsp sea salt
* * 1 tsp smoky paprika

## Directions

1. 1. In a skillet heat the olive oil till warm and shimmering. Cook outside.
2. 2. Add habaneros, jalapenos, tabascos, garlic, and shallot to the pan and cook about 5 minutes, till softened.
3. 3. Add the hot paprika and cook about 1 minute till fragrant.
4. 4. Add the habanero mixture to a food processor, add the rest of the ingredients and pulse till it reaches desired consistency. Adjust consistency with water as needed.

_Enjoy!_
