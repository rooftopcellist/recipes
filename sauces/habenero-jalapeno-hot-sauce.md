# Habanero Jalape単o Hot Sauce

![Habanero Jalape単o Hot Sauce](images/habenero-jalapeno-hot-sauce.jpeg)

## Ingredients

* 10 habanero peppers
* 5 jalape単o peppers
* 4 cloves garlic
* 1/2 cup white vinegar
* 1/4 cup water
* 1 tablespoon salt
* 1 tablespoon sugar (optional, to balance heat)

## Directions

1. Wash and remove stems from all peppers. For a milder sauce, remove some or all of the seeds.
2. Peel the garlic cloves.
3. Combine peppers, garlic, vinegar, water, and salt in a saucepan.
4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until peppers are soft.
5. Let cool slightly, then blend until smooth using a blender or immersion blender.
6. Strain through a fine mesh sieve for a smoother consistency (optional).
7. Taste and adjust salt and sugar as needed.
8. Pour into sterilized bottles and refrigerate.

## Notes

* **Safety:** Wear gloves when handling habaneros! Avoid touching your face or eyes.
* **Heat level:** Adjust the ratio of habaneros to jalape単os based on your heat preference.
* **Storage:** Keeps refrigerated for up to 6 months.

_Enjoy responsibly - this one packs some heat!_
